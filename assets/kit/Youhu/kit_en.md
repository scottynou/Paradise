- Internal skill id: `1002402`
- Name: `Scroll Divination`

Lucky Draw

Only one Antique can exist at a time, and the newly drawn Antique will replace the existing one.

### Forte Circuit

- Internal skill id: `1002407`
- Name: `Poetic Essence`

Description

At four Auspices, hold the Normal Attack button to release Poetic Essence, dealing Glacio DMG, considered as Resonance Skill DMG, while restoring HP for all nearby party members.
Youhu can hold up to four Auspices.

Poetic Essence
Deal Glacio DMG to enemies within the range and additionally obtain one of the following effects based on the Auspice combination:
- Free Verse: Four different types Auspices. Additionally reduce the Vibration Strength of hit enemies.
- Antithesis: A pair of Auspices. Increase Poetic Essence's DMG by {0}.
- Double Pun: Two pairs of Auspices. Additionally restore HP for all nearby party members.
- Triplet: Three identical Auspices. Increase Poetic Essence's DMG by {1}.
- Perfect Rhyme: Four identical Auspices. Simultaneously activate the effects of Free Verse, Double Pun, and Triplet.
Casting Poetic Essence removes all Auspices.

Auspice
- Youhu gains an Auspice through Antique Appraisal. Use Basic Attack while holding an Antique to receive the corresponding Auspice.
- Youhu unlocks Antiques by casting Resonance Skill Scroll Divination, Intro Skill Scroll of Wonders, Heavy Attack Frostfall, Resonance Liberation Fortune's Favor, and Dodge Counter.,

Short Summary

At four Auspices, hold Basic Attack to cast Poetic Essence, dealing Glacio DMG while healing all nearby party members.
Youhu can hold up to 4 Auspices.

Poetic Essence
Deal Glacio DMG and also gain one of the following effects based on Auspice combinations:
- Free Verse: 4 unique Auspices additionally reduce the Vibration Strength of enemies.
- Antithesis: 1 pair of Auspices increase DMG by {0}.
- Double Pun: 2 pairs of Auspices additionally heal all nearby party members.
- Triplet: 3 identical Auspices increase DMG by {1}.
- Perfect Rhyme: 4 identical Auspices activate the effects of Free Verse, Double Pun, and Triplet all at once.
Casting Poetic Essence removes all Auspices.

Auspice
Youhu gains Auspice through Antique Appraisal.
